About Tocal

TOCAL is a hyperlocal & last-mile delivery service platform. We provide a dedicated fleet of delivery executives with Electric Vehicles for Quick-commerce, E-commerce, D2C, online/offline Retail companies, SMEs etc.

Our trained and reliable delivery executives make quick and safe delivery of your packages not only limited to Groceries, E-commerce, Food, Medicines etc. Our full-stack technology platform ensures an end-to-end efficient delivery management every day; every delivery. While doing this, we also make sure to provide delightful experience of your brand till the last touchpoint of delivery
